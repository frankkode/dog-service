About Us

DOOGY HUB is a Swedish non-profit organisation that receives, nurtures / rehabilitates dogs that are threatened by killing due to various reasons, neglected Dogs or those Dogs that have no one who can take care of them and find new homes for them.

We believe that all animals are entitled to a life and we want to help them as much as possible.

If you are interested in taking care of a Dog from us, call us and book the time.

We are more than happy to help you who have a Dog you can no longer find new home for, all in order to avoid killing a healthy Dog, contact us, we have our own transport.

Train with us.

At DOGGY HUB On demand We offer exceptional one on one modern dog life style & training with an emphasis on proven, ethical and science based methods for free. We are fully qualified with years of experience, all of our works are 100% free of charge and satisfaction is guaranteed. Always done with kindness, Our work is about empowering owners with knowledge and skillset to achieve the great behaviours you want from your dog.
